# Points / Puntos

> **level 0** · *data* · sources: th_design_frames · th_validation

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** [[georeferenced-information|Georeferenced]] locations, often representing individual [[samples|sample]] units, used for [[training|training]] or validating [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]] models.
- **ES.** Ubicaciones georreferenciadas, que a menudo representan unidades de muestra individuales, utilizadas para entrenar o validar modelos de [[remote-sensing|teledetección]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[vector|Vector]] — A vector can represent a point, line, or polygon, enabling spatial analysis of discrete features.
