# Spectral bands / Bandas espectrales

> **level 5** · *data* · sources: th_remote_sensing

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Discrete [[wavelength|wavelength]] intervals in the [[electromagnetic-spectrum|electromagnetic spectrum]] recorded by a [[sensors|sensor]], such as visible, [[near-infrared|near-infrared]], or shortwave infrared [[bands|bands]], used to differentiate [[earth|Earth]] surface materials.
- **ES.** Intervalos discretos de [[wavelength|longitud de onda]] en el [[electromagnetic-spectrum|espectro electromagnético]] registrados por un sensor, como [[bands|bandas]] visibles, [[near-infrared|infrarrojo cercano]] o infrarrojo de onda corta, utilizados para diferenciar materiales de la [[earth|superficie terrestre]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[bands|Bands]] — *data*
    - **EN.** Bands are discrete spectral channels in satellite imagery that capture reflectance in specific wavelength ranges.
    - **ES.** Las bandas son canales espectrales discretos en las imágenes satelitales que capturan reflectancia en rangos de longitud de onda específicos.
- [[indices|Indices]] — *index*
    - **EN.** Indices are computed by mathematically combining two or more spectral bands.
    - **ES.** Los índices se calculan combinando matemáticamente dos o más bandas espectrales.
- [[satellite-sensors|Satellite sensors]] — *sensor*
    - **EN.** Satellite sensors capture data in distinct wavelength intervals known as spectral bands.
    - **ES.** Los sensores satelitales capturan datos en intervalos de longitud de onda distintos conocidos como bandas espectrales.
- [[wavelength|Wavelength]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** Each spectral band corresponds to a narrow range of wavelengths in the electromagnetic spectrum.
    - **ES.** Cada banda espectral corresponde a un rango estrecho de longitudes de onda en el espectro electromagnético.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[spectral-index|Spectral index]] — Spectral index is a mathematical combination of two or more spectral bands to enhance a specific surface property.
- [[red-reflectance|Red reflectance]] — The red spectral band (around 600-700 nm) measures red reflectance from the Earth's surface.
- [[spectral-channels|Spectral channels]] — Spectral channels are the discrete wavelength intervals recorded by a sensor, also called bands.
- [[spectral-indices|Spectral Indices]] — Spectral indices are mathematical combinations of two or more spectral bands to highlight specific surface properties.
