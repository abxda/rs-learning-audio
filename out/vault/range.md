# Range / Rango

> **level 0** · *primitive* · sources: th_remote_sensing · th_data_sources

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The span of wavelengths ([[spectral-range|spectral range]]) or the [[spatial-area|spatial area]] (coverage) that a [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]] [[sensors|sensor]] can detect.
- **ES.** El intervalo de longitudes de onda ([[spectral-range|rango espectral]]) o el [[spatial-area|área espacial]] ([[cover|cobertura]]) que un sensor de [[remote-sensing|teledetección]] puede detectar.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[spectral-range|Spectral Range]] — Range is a shortened term often used to refer to spectral range or spatial coverage of a sensor.
