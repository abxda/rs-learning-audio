# Phenology / Fenología

> **level 3** · *phenomenon* · sources: th_lucc

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The [[study|study]] of recurring biological events in plant life cycles, such as emergence, flowering, and senescence, often monitored via [[satellite-image-time-series|satellite time series]] to track [[crop|crop]] [[development|development]].
- **ES.** [[study|Estudio]] de eventos biológicos recurrentes en los ciclos de vida de las plantas, como la emergencia, la floración y la senescencia, a menudo monitoreado mediante series temporales de [[satellite|satélite]] para rastrear el [[development|desarrollo]] del [[crop|cultivo]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[crop|Crop]] — *phenomenon*
    - **EN.** Phenology studies the timing of recurring biological events in crop life cycles, such as flowering.
    - **ES.** La fenología estudia el momento de los eventos biológicos recurrentes en los ciclos de vida de los cultivos, como la floración.
- [[time|Time]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** By observing vegetation over time, phenology tracks seasonal changes in plant development stages.
    - **ES.** Al observar la vegetación a lo largo del tiempo, la fenología rastrea los cambios estacionales en las etapas de desarrollo de las plantas.
- [[crop-development-stage|Crop development stage]] — *phenomenon*
    - **EN.** A crop development stage is a distinct phenological phase in a crop's growth cycle.
    - **ES.** Una etapa de desarrollo del cultivo es una fase fenológica distinta en el ciclo de crecimiento de un cultivo.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[temporal-pattern|Temporal pattern]] — Crop phenology drives the temporal pattern of vegetation indices over a growing season.
- [[phenological-patterns|Phenological patterns]] — Phenological patterns are the repetitive sequences of plant growth stages observed in phenology, such as leaf emergence.
- [[phenological-stage|Phenological stage]] — A phenological stage is a distinct phase in the growth cycle of a crop, such as flowering or maturity.
