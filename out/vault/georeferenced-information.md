# Georeferenced information / Información georreferenciada

> **level 2** · *data* · sources: th_alignment

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** [[data|Data]] that has been assigned a location on the [[earth|Earth]]'s surface using a [[coordinate-reference-system|coordinate reference system]], enabling [[spatial-analysis|spatial analysis]] and [[mapping|mapping]].
- **ES.** [[data|Datos]] que han sido asignados a una ubicación en la superficie de la [[land|Tierra]] mediante un [[coordinate-reference-system|sistema de referencia de coordenadas]], lo que permite el [[spatial-analysis|análisis espacial]] y la cartografía.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[information|Information]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** Georeferenced information is data linked to a specific location on Earth's surface.
    - **ES.** La información georreferenciada son datos vinculados a una ubicación específica en la superficie de la Tierra.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[coordinate-system|Coordinate system]] — A coordinate system provides the reference for georeferenced information, linking data to locations.
- [[geographic-information-systems|Geographic information systems]] — GIS integrates georeferenced information layers such as satellite imagery, field boundaries, and survey records.
- [[spatial-information|Spatial information]] — Spatial information is any data referenced to locations, i.e., georeferenced information.

- [[geographic-data|Geographic data]] — Geographic data is essentially georeferenced information, tied to coordinates.
