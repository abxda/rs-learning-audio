# Neural networks / Redes neuronales

> **level 4** · *method* · sources: th_machine_learning · th_parcel_extraction

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Computational models inspired by biological neural networks, consisting of interconnected [[layers|layers]] of nodes (neurons) that learn [[patterns|patterns]] from [[data|data]] through [[training|training]].
- **ES.** Modelos computacionales inspirados en redes neuronales biológicas, que consisten en [[layers|capas]] interconectadas de nodos (neuronas) que aprenden [[patterns|patrones]] de [[data|datos]] a través del [[training|entrenamiento]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[machine-learning|Machine learning]] — *method*
    - **EN.** Neural networks are a class of machine learning algorithms inspired by biological neurons.
    - **ES.** Las redes neuronales son una clase de algoritmos de aprendizaje automático inspirados en neuronas biológicas.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[mlp-models|MLP models]] — MLP models are a specific type of neural network with fully connected layers.
- [[deep-learning|Deep learning]] — It extends neural networks by adding many hidden layers that automatically extract complex patterns from data.
- [[hidden-layers|Hidden layers]] — Hidden layers are intermediate layers in a neural network between input and output that transform features.
