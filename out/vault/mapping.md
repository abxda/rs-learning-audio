# Mapping / Mapeo

> **level 4** · *method* · sources: ct_zimbabwe#crop-classification

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The [[process|process]] of producing thematic maps, such as [[crop-types|crop type]] or [[land-cover|land cover]] maps, from [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]] [[data|data]].
- **ES.** El [[process|proceso]] de producir mapas temáticos, como mapas de tipos de [[crop|cultivo]] o de [[land-cover|cobertura del suelo]], a partir de [[data|datos]] de [[remote-sensing|teledetección]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[classification|Classification]] — *method*
    - **EN.** Mapping produces thematic maps by applying classification to satellite imagery.
    - **ES.** El mapeo produce mapas temáticos aplicando clasificación a las imágenes satelitales.
- [[map|Map]] — *product*
    - **EN.** Mapping is the process of producing thematic maps from remote sensing data.
    - **ES.** El mapeo es el proceso de producir mapas temáticos a partir de datos de teledetección.

[[data|Data]] → [[samples|Samples]] → [[classification|Classification]] → [[map|Map]] → [[mapping|Mapping]]

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[detailed-mapping|Detailed mapping]] — Detailed mapping extends general mapping by using high-resolution imagery and object-based methods to produce fine-scale crop maps.
- [[national-scale-mapping|National scale mapping]] — National scale mapping applies mapping techniques to produce country-wide thematic maps.
- [[early-season-crop-yield-mapping|Early season crop yield mapping]] — Mapping techniques produce spatial yield estimates from satellite imagery and models.
- [[crop-mapping|Crop mapping]] — Crop mapping is the process of producing thematic maps showing crop types, building on the general mapping concept.
- [[land-use-mapping|Land use mapping]] — Land use mapping produces themes showing socioeconomic functions, building on general mapping processes.
