# Land / Tierra

> **level 1** · *phenomenon* · sources: th_lucc

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The [[earth|Earth]]'s surface, including its [[cover|cover]] ([[vegetation|vegetation]], water) and use ([[agriculture|agriculture]], urban), which is classified and monitored for environmental and [[statistical|statistical]] purposes.
- **ES.** Superficie de la Tierra, incluyendo su [[cover|cobertura]] ([[vegetation|vegetación]], agua) y uso ([[agriculture|agricultura]], urbano), que se clasifica y monitorea para fines ambientales y [[statisticians|estadísticos]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[area|Area]] — *metric*
    - **EN.** Area quantifies the spatial extent of land cover classes or agricultural fields.
    - **ES.** El área cuantifica la extensión espacial de las clases de cobertura del suelo o los campos agrícolas.
- [[classes|Classes]] — *data*
    - **EN.** Classes are discrete categories assigned to land cover types in a classification scheme.
    - **ES.** Las clases son categorías discretas asignadas a tipos de cobertura del suelo en un esquema de clasificación.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[earth|Earth]] — Earth is the planet, including its land cover and surface, as observed by satellite imagery.
- [[field|Field]] — A field is an area of land cultivated with a single crop or managed as a unit.
- [[vegetation|Vegetation]] — Vegetation is plant cover on Earth's surface, a key land cover type monitored via remote sensing.
- [[fao-land-cover-classification-system|FAO Land Cover Classification System]] — The FAO Land Cover Classification System provides a standardized framework to classify land cover types.
- [[agriculture|Agriculture]] — Agriculture uses land as a resource for cultivation and livestock production.
- [[cropland|Cropland]] — Cropland is land that is used for growing crops, distinguishing it from other land cover types.
- [[soil|Soil]] — Soil is the upper layer of the Earth's land surface that supports plant growth.
- [[land-cover-classes|Land cover classes]] — Land cover classes categorize the Earth's surface, such as forest, water, or crop, based on satellite observations.
- [[cover|Cover]] — Land cover is the observed biophysical material on the Earth's surface.
- [[environment|Environment]] — Land is a key component of the environment monitored through satellite imagery.
- [[landscapes|Landscapes]] — Landscapes are geographic areas composed of diverse land cover types such as farm fields and forests.
- [[land-classes|Land classes]] — Land classes partition the Earth's surface into categories based on its cover or use.
- [[agricultural-land-areas|Agricultural land areas]] — Land areas dedicated to farming are agricultural land areas, classified by use and cover.
- [[land-classification|Land classification]] — Land classification assigns labels to land cover types observed on the Earth's surface.
- [[land-use-mapping|Land use mapping]] — Land use mapping classifies land according to its use, based on understanding the Earth's surface.
