# Indices / Índices

> **level 0** · *index* · sources: th_remote_sensing

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Derived spectral measures computed from two or more [[satellite-imagery|satellite image]] [[bands|bands]], such as [[ndvi|NDVI]], used to highlight specific properties like [[vegetation|vegetation]] vigor or moisture content.
- **ES.** Medidas espectrales derivadas calculadas a partir de dos o más [[bands|bandas]] de [[satellite-imagery|imágenes satelitales]], como el [[ndvi|NDVI]], utilizadas para resaltar propiedades específicas como el vigor de la [[vegetation|vegetación]] o el contenido de humedad.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[spectral-bands|Spectral bands]] — Indices are computed by mathematically combining two or more spectral bands.
- [[satellite-imagery|Satellite imagery]] — Satellite imagery provides the spectral bands used to calculate vegetation indices.
- [[indicators|Indicators]] — Indicators such as NDVI are spectral indices that highlight vegetation properties.
