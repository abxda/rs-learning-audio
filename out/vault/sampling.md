# Sampling / Muestreo

> **level 4** · *method* · sources: th_validation · th_design_frames

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A [[methods|method]] for selecting a subset of units from a [[population|population]] to estimate characteristics of the whole, often using [[probability|probability]]-based designs like [[stratified-random-sampling|stratified random sampling]] to ensure [[unbiased-area-estimates|unbiased estimates]].
- **ES.** Método para seleccionar un subconjunto de unidades de una [[population|población]] con el fin de estimar características del total, utilizando a menudo diseños basados en [[probability|probabilidad]], como el [[stratified-random-sampling|muestreo aleatorio estratificado]], para garantizar [[estimates|estimaciones]] insesgadas.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[design|Design]] — *method*
    - **EN.** Design specifies the sampling strategy to collect representative data for estimation.
    - **ES.** El diseño especifica la estrategia de muestreo para recolectar datos representativos para la estimación.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[population|Population]] — Sampling selects a subset of units from a population to estimate characteristics while reducing cost.
- [[statistical-methods|Statistical methods]] — They rely on sampling theory to select representative units and draw inferences about the population.
- [[design-estimator|Design estimator]] — The design estimator uses the probability-based sampling scheme to produce unbiased population estimates.
- [[survey-approach|Survey approach]] — The survey approach uses a probability sample of ground observations to make unbiased population estimates.
- [[sample-design|Sample design]] — Sample design is the detailed plan for how sampling is implemented, including selection methods.
- [[representativeness|Representativeness]] — Sampling designs aim to achieve representativeness so that sample statistics can be generalized to the whole area.
- [[sample-based-estimators|Sample-based estimators]] — The sampling design determines the properties of the sample-based estimator, such as unbiasedness and variance.
- [[stratified-random-sampling|Stratified random sampling]] — Stratified random sampling extends simple random sampling by partitioning the population into homogeneous subgroups.
- [[sampling-frame|Sampling frame]] — A sampling frame is the list or spatial structure from which a sample is selected.
- [[sampling-strategies|Sampling strategies]] — Sampling strategies are plans for selecting units from a population to estimate characteristics.
