# Statistical / Estadístico

> **level 0** · *primitive* · sources: introduction · th_validation

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Relating to mathematical [[methods|methods]] for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting [[data|data]] to ensure validity, [[reliability|reliability]], and [[inference|inference]] about populations from [[samples|samples]].
- **ES.** Relativo a [[methods|métodos]] matemáticos para recopilar, analizar e interpretar [[data|datos]] con el fin de garantizar la validez, [[reliability|fiabilidad]] e [[inference|inferencia]] sobre poblaciones a partir de [[samples|muestras]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[statistical-methods|Statistical methods]] — The term 'statistical' refers to methods grounded in probability and inference, derived from statistical methods.
