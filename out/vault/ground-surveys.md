# Ground surveys / Encuestas de campo

> **level 3** · *method* · sources: th_alignment

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** [[field|Field]]-based [[data-collection|data collection]] activities, such as measuring [[crop-yield|crop yield]] or recording [[land-cover|land cover]], used to train and validate [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]] models.
- **ES.** Actividades de [[data-collection|recolección de datos]] en [[field|campo]], como medir el [[crop-yield|rendimiento de cultivos]] o registrar la [[land-cover|cobertura del suelo]], utilizadas para entrenar y validar modelos de [[remote-sensing|teledetección]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[field|Field]] — *phenomenon*
    - **EN.** Ground surveys visit agricultural fields to record crop types, phenology, and other attributes.
    - **ES.** Las encuestas de campo visitan campos agrícolas para registrar tipos de cultivo, fenología y otros atributos.
- [[survey|Survey]] — *method*
    - **EN.** Ground surveys are field-based data collection campaigns that provide ground truth for remote sensing.
    - **ES.** Las encuestas de campo son campañas de recolección de datos basadas en el terreno que proporcionan verdad terreno para la teledetección.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[surveying|Surveying]] — It often involves field-based data collection, synonymous with ground surveys.
- [[field-data-collection|Field data collection]] — It is a systematic process of recording ground surveys to provide reference data for remote sensing analysis.
- [[field-samples|Field samples]] — Field samples are the primary output of ground surveys, used for training and validation.
- [[fao-situ-quality-framework|FAO situ quality framework]] — The FAO situ quality framework provides criteria for evaluating ground survey data quality.
