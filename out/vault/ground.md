# Ground / Terreno

> **level 3** · *phenomenon* · sources: th_design_frames

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Ground refers to [[in-situ-observations|in-situ observations]] or measurements collected on the [[earth|Earth]]'s surface, such as [[crop-types|crop type]] and [[yield|yield]], used as [[reference-datasets|reference data]] for calibrating and validating [[satellite|satellite]]-based products.
- **ES.** El terreno se refiere a observaciones o mediciones in situ recopiladas en la superficie de la [[land|Tierra]], como [[type|tipo]] de [[crop|cultivo]] y [[yield|rendimiento]], utilizadas como [[ground-truth-data|datos de referencia]] para calibrar y validar productos basados en [[satellite|satélite]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[observation|Observation]] — *data*
    - **EN.** Ground observations are measurements taken directly on the Earth's surface, such as crop type and yield data.
    - **ES.** Las observaciones de campo son mediciones tomadas directamente en la superficie de la Tierra, como tipo de cultivo y datos de rendimiento.
- [[reference|Reference]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** Reference data consists of ground observations used to label satellite image samples.
    - **ES.** Los datos de referencia consisten en observaciones de campo utilizadas para etiquetar muestras de imágenes satelitales.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[ground-observations|Ground observations]] — Ground observations are specific measurements taken on the Earth's surface, synonymous with ground data.
- [[ground-truth-2|Ground truth]] — Ground truth data are direct observations of actual conditions on the Earth's surface used for validation.
- [[ground-measurements|Ground measurements]] — Ground measurements are in-situ observations collected on the Earth's surface, such as crop yield or type.
- [[extensive-ground-samples|Extensive ground samples]] — Extensive ground samples are many in-situ observations, building from the ground concept of field data.
