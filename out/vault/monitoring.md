# Monitoring / Monitoreo

> **level 2** · *method* · sources: introduction · uav_crop_growth

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The systematic and repeated [[observation|observation]] of a phenomenon over [[time|time]] to detect changes, trends, or anomalies.
- **ES.** La [[observation|observación]] sistemática y repetida de un fenómeno a lo largo del [[time|tiempo]] para detectar cambios, tendencias o anomalías.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[time|Time]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** Monitoring involves systematic, repeated observation over time to detect changes or trends.
    - **ES.** El monitoreo implica una observación sistemática y repetida a lo largo del tiempo para detectar cambios o tendencias.
- [[observation|Observation]] — *data*
    - **EN.** Monitoring relies on a series of observations at regular intervals to track conditions.
    - **ES.** El monitoreo depende de una serie de observaciones a intervalos regulares para rastrear las condiciones.

[[data|Data]] → [[observation|Observation]] → [[monitoring|Monitoring]]

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[agricultural-monitoring|Agricultural monitoring]] — Agricultural monitoring applies systematic observation methods to track crop conditions and changes over the growing season.
- [[operational-crop-monitoring|Operational crop monitoring]] — Operational crop monitoring applies systematic satellite observation and models routinely to track crops.
- [[vegetation-monitoring|Vegetation monitoring]] — Vegetation monitoring applies systematic repeated observation specifically to plant cover.
- [[continuous-monitoring|Continuous monitoring]] — Continuous monitoring systematically observes land surface changes at each satellite revisit.
- [[environmental-monitoring|Environmental monitoring]] — Environmental monitoring uses satellite time series to track changes in vegetation, water, and soil.
- [[near-real-time|Near real-time]] — Near real‑time capability is essential for operational monitoring of crop conditions as the growing season progresses.
- [[large-scale-agricultural-monitoring|Large scale agricultural monitoring]] — Large-scale agricultural monitoring extends systematic observation to regional or national levels.
