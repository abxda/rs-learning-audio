# Kappa / Kappa

> **level 0** · *primitive*

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Short for [[cohen-s-kappa|Cohen's Kappa]]; a chance-corrected agreement [[statistics|statistic]] for categorical [[data|data]].
- **ES.** Abreviatura de [[cohen-s-kappa|Kappa de Cohen]]; un [[statistical|estadístico]] de acuerdo corregido por azar para [[data|datos]] categóricos.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[inter-rater-agreement|Inter-rater agreement]] — Inter-rater agreement is often quantified using Kappa statistics to account for chance agreement.
