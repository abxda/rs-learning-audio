# Echo / Eco

> **level 0** · *primitive*

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The reflected signal received by a [[radar|radar]] or lidar [[sensors|sensor]] after interacting with a target.
- **ES.** La señal reflejada recibida por un sensor de [[radar|radar]] o lidar después de interactuar con un objetivo.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[radar-signal|Radar signal]] — A radar signal is the transmitted pulse, and the echo is its reflection from the ground.
