# Annotator / Anotador

> **level 0** · *primitive*

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A person who [[label|labels]] or marks [[ground-truth-2|ground truth]] [[data|data]] for [[training|training]] or [[validation|validation]].
- **ES.** Persona que [[label|etiqueta]] o marca [[ground-truth-data|datos de verdad terreno]] para [[training|entrenamiento]] o [[validation|validación]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[inter-rater-agreement|Inter-rater agreement]] — Inter-rater agreement measures consistency among annotators labeling the same samples for reference data.
