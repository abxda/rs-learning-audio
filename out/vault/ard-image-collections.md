# ARD image collections / colecciones de imágenes ARD

> **level 0** · *data* · sources: th_data_sources#ard-image-collections

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Collections of [[satellite|satellite]] [[images|images]] processed to [[analysis-ready-data|Analysis Ready Data]] (ARD) standards, meaning they are geometrically, radiometrically, and atmospherically corrected for direct use.
- **ES.** [[satellite-image-collections|Colecciones de imágenes satelitales]] procesadas según los estándares de [[analysis-ready-data|Datos Listos para Análisis]] (ARD), corregidas geométrica, radiométrica y atmosféricamente para uso directo.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[analysis-ready-data|Analysis Ready Data]] — ARD image collections are sets of satellite images processed to Analysis Ready Data standards.
- [[satellite-image-collections|Satellite image collections]] — Satellite image collections can be stored as ARD image collections, processed to analysis-ready standards.
