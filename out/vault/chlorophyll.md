# Chlorophyll / Clorofila

> **level 0** · *primitive*

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Green pigment in plants, measured via [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]] to assess [[vegetation-health|vegetation health]].
- **ES.** Pigmento verde en las plantas, medido mediante [[remote-sensing|teledetección]] para evaluar la [[vegetation-health|salud de la vegetación]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[chlorophyll-absorption|Chlorophyll absorption]] — Chlorophyll absorption refers to the uptake of light energy by plant chlorophyll molecules.
- [[vegetation-health|Vegetation health]] — Chlorophyll content is linked to vegetation health, as it drives photosynthesis.
