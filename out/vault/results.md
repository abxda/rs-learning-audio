# Results / Resultados

> **level 0** · *primitive* · sources: th_validation

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Outputs of [[data-processing|data processing]] or [[analysis|analysis]], such as [[classified-map|classified maps]], [[accuracy-metrics|accuracy metrics]], or [[area-statistics|area estimates]], used for reporting and decision-making.
- **ES.** Resultados del [[processing|procesamiento]] o [[data-analysis|análisis de datos]], como mapas clasificados, [[accuracy-metrics|métricas de precisión]] o [[estimates|estimaciones]] de [[area|área]], utilizados para informes y toma de decisiones.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[analysis|Analysis]] — Results are the outputs of analysis, such as classified maps or accuracy metrics.
- [[figure|Figure]] — A figure visually presents results, such as a classified map or accuracy chart.
- [[product|Product]] — A product is a results dataset, such as a crop type map or yield estimate, generated for end users.
- [[classification-results|Classification results]] — Classification results are the outputs produced by a classification algorithm.
