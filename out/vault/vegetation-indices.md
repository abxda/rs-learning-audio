# Vegetation indices / Índices de vegetación

> **level 4** · *index* · sources: th_remote_sensing

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Mathematical combinations of [[spectral-bands|spectral bands]], such as [[ndvi|NDVI]] and EVI, designed to enhance [[vegetation|vegetation]] signal and assess [[crop|crop]] health, [[biomass|biomass]], and [[phenological-stage|phenological stage]].
- **ES.** Combinaciones matemáticas de [[bands|bandas espectrales]], como [[ndvi|NDVI]] y EVI, diseñadas para mejorar la señal de [[vegetation|vegetación]] y evaluar la salud del [[crop|cultivo]], la [[biomass|biomasa]] y la [[phenological-stage|etapa fenológica]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[vegetation|Vegetation]] — *phenomenon*
    - **EN.** Vegetation indices use spectral combinations to quantify green biomass and plant condition.
    - **ES.** Los índices de vegetación usan combinaciones espectrales para cuantificar la biomasa verde y la condición de la planta.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[satellite-vegetation-indices|Satellite vegetation indices]] — Satellite vegetation indices are mathematical combinations of spectral bands computed from satellite sensors.
- [[cgi-algorithm|CGI algorithm]] — By combining multiple vegetation indices, the CGI algorithm captures different aspects of crop growth.
- [[soil-vegetation-index|Soil Vegetation Index]] — A soil-vegetation index (e.g., OSAVI) minimizes soil background influence to better estimate vegetation cover.
- [[spectral-indices|Spectral Indices]] — Vegetation indices are spectral indices designed to highlight vegetation health and cover.
- [[soil-moisture|Soil moisture]] — Vegetation indices help infer soil moisture by monitoring plant water stress indirectly.
