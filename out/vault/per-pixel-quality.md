# Per-pixel quality / Calidad por píxel

> **level 0** · *primitive*

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** [[assessment|Assessment]] of each image [[pixels|pixel]]'s [[reliability|reliability]] or clarity, often from [[cloud|cloud]] or [[sensors|sensor]] flags.
- **ES.** [[assessment|Evaluación]] de la [[reliability|fiabilidad]] o claridad de cada píxel de una imagen, a menudo basada en banderas de nubes o del sensor.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[quality-band|Quality band]] — A quality band is a raster layer providing per-pixel quality information for satellite imagery.
