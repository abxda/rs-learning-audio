# Tillering samples / Muestras de macollamiento

> **level 4** · *data* · sources: cy_colombia#noisy-sample-detection

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** [[training-dataset|Training data]] [[points|points]] representing the tillering [[phenological-stage|phenological stage]], where the [[crop|crop]] develops side shoots; these [[samples|samples]] may show spectral confusion with adjacent [[stages|stages]].
