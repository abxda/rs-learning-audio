# Information / Información

> **level 1** · *primitive* · sources: th_lucc#problem-statement

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Meaningful [[data|data]] derived from [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]] after [[processing|processing]] and interpretation, such as [[crop-types|crop type]] [[label|labels]] or [[yield|yield estimates]].
- **ES.** [[data|Datos]] significativos derivados de la [[remote-sensing|teledetección]] después del [[processing|procesamiento]] e interpretación, como etiquetas de tipos de [[crop|cultivo]] o [[estimates|estimaciones]] de [[yield|rendimiento]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[data|Data]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** Information is meaningful data derived from processing and interpreting remote sensing data.
    - **ES.** La información son datos significativos derivados del procesamiento e interpretación de datos de teledetección.

[[data|Data]] → [[information|Information]]

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[georeferenced-information|Georeferenced information]] — Georeferenced information is data linked to a specific location on Earth's surface.
- [[geoinformation|Geoinformation]] — Geoinformation is information that has geographic location and attributes derived from Earth observation.
