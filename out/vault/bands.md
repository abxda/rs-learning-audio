# Bands / Bandas

> **level 0** · *data* · sources: th_remote_sensing · th_data_cubes

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Discrete [[spectral-channels|spectral channels]] in [[satellite-imagery|satellite imagery]] that capture [[reflectance|reflectance]] in specific [[wavelength|wavelength]] ranges, such as visible, [[near-infrared|near-infrared]], and shortwave infrared, used to compute [[vegetation-indices|vegetation indices]].
- **ES.** [[spectral-channels|Canales espectrales]] discretos en [[satellite-imagery|imágenes satelitales]] que capturan la [[reflectance|reflectancia]] en rangos de [[wavelength|longitud de onda]] específicos, como el visible, [[near-infrared|infrarrojo cercano]] e infrarrojo de onda corta, utilizados para calcular [[vegetation-indices|índices de vegetación]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[spectral-bands|Spectral bands]] — Bands are discrete spectral channels in satellite imagery that capture reflectance in specific wavelength ranges.
- [[ndvi|NDVI]] — NDVI uses the near-infrared and red bands from satellite imagery to compute its index value.
