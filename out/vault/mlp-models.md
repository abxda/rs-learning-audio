# MLP models / modelos MLP

> **level 5** · *method* · sources: th_machine_learning

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A [[type|type]] of feedforward artificial [[neural-networks|neural network]] consisting of an [[input|input]] layer, one or more [[hidden-layers|hidden layers]], and an output layer, used for [[classification|classification]] and [[regression|regression]].
- **ES.** Un [[type|tipo]] de red neuronal artificial feedforward que consta de una capa de [[input|entrada]], una o más [[hidden-layers|capas ocultas]] y una capa de salida, utilizada para [[classification|clasificación]] y [[regression|regresión]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[neural-networks|Neural networks]] — *method*
    - **EN.** MLP models are a specific type of neural network with fully connected layers.
    - **ES.** Los modelos MLP son un tipo específico de red neuronal con capas completamente conectadas.
