# Study area / Área de estudio

> **level 0** · *data* · sources: ct_chile

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The [[regions|geographic region]] selected for [[analysis|analysis]], defined by [[boundaries|boundaries]] and characteristics relevant to the [[research|research]] [[objectives|objectives]].
- **ES.** La región geográfica seleccionada para el [[analysis|análisis]], definida por [[boundaries|límites]] y características relevantes para los [[objectives|objetivos]] de la [[study|investigación]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[experimental-area|Experimental Area]] — An experimental area is a specific geographic region selected for conducting a remote sensing experiment.
- [[boundaries|Boundaries]] — A study area is delineated by boundaries that define its spatial extent for remote sensing.
