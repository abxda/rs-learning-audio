# Data Systems / Sistemas de datos

> **level 1** · *software* · sources: th_data_sources · ct_zimbabwe

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Integrated [[platforms|platforms]] for storing, [[processing|processing]], and analyzing [[earth-observation|Earth observation data]] at [[scale|scale]], such as Digital [[earth|Earth]] Africa or Sen4Stat.
- **ES.** [[platforms|Plataformas]] integradas para almacenar, procesar y analizar [[data|datos]] de [[earth-observation|observación de la Tierra]] a [[scale|escala]], como Digital Earth Africa o Sen4Stat.
