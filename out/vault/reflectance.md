# Reflectance / Reflectancia

> **level 3** · *data* · sources: th_remote_sensing

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The proportion of incident light that is reflected from a surface, typically expressed as a value between 0 and 1. It is a fundamental [[measure|measure]] in [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]] for analyzing surface properties.
- **ES.** Proporción de la luz incidente que se refleja desde una superficie, típicamente expresada como un valor entre 0 y 1. Es una [[measure|medida]] fundamental en [[remote-sensing|teledetección]] para analizar las propiedades de la superficie.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[values|Values]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** Pixels in satellite images store digital numbers that are converted to reflectance values.
    - **ES.** Los píxeles en imágenes satelitales almacenan números digitales que se convierten en valores de reflectancia.
- [[spectral-reflectance|Spectral reflectance]] — *data*
    - **EN.** Reflectance is the ratio of reflected to incident radiation, often derived from spectral reflectance.
    - **ES.** La reflectancia es la relación entre la radiación reflejada y la incidente, a menudo derivada de la reflectancia espectral.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[spectral-index|Spectral index]] — Reflectance values from spectral bands are used to compute spectral indices that highlight vegetation or other features.
- [[red-reflectance|Red reflectance]] — Red reflectance is the portion of incident light reflected in the red spectral region, a key input for vegetation indices.
- [[spectral-indices|Spectral Indices]] — Spectral indices use surface reflectance values from different bands, such as NDVI from red and NIR bands.
- [[surface-reflectance|Surface reflectance]] — Surface reflectance is the reflectance measured at the Earth's surface after removing atmospheric effects.
