# Parameters / Parámetros

> **level 1** · *primitive* · sources: th_machine_learning

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Configurable [[values|values]] that control the behavior of a [[model|model]] or [[algorithm|algorithm]], such as [[machine-learning|learning]] rate or [[number|number]] of trees.
- **ES.** [[values|Valores]] configurables que controlan el comportamiento de un [[model|modelo]] o [[algorithm|algoritmo]], como la tasa de aprendizaje o el [[number|número]] de árboles.
