# Study / Estudio

> **level 1** · *primitive* · sources: introduction · ct_chile

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A systematic investigation designed to develop or contribute to generalizable knowledge, such as a [[case-study|case study]] or experiment.
- **ES.** Una investigación sistemática diseñada para desarrollar o contribuir al conocimiento generalizable, como un [[case-study|estudio de caso]] o experimento.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[area|Area]] — *metric*
    - **EN.** A study is a systematic investigation within a defined area to develop or contribute knowledge.
    - **ES.** Un estudio es una investigación sistemática dentro de un área definida para desarrollar o contribuir al conocimiento.
- [[case|Case]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** A case is a specific example or instance of a study, illustrating the application of methods.
    - **ES.** Un caso es un ejemplo o instancia específica de un estudio, que ilustra la aplicación de métodos.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[research|Research]] — Research advances knowledge by conducting systematic studies and experiments to develop or refine methods.
- [[project|Project]] — A project is a planned undertaking with specific objectives, larger than a study, often involving multiple steps.
- [[experimental-area|Experimental Area]] — This area serves as the spatial domain for a case study or pilot investigation.
- [[research-areas|Research areas]] — Research areas are specific geographic or thematic domains where a study or field experiment is conducted.
- [[case-study|Case study]] — A case study is a detailed investigation of a specific instance or application within the larger study.
- [[case-studies|Case studies]] — Case studies are detailed investigations of specific examples, illustrating methods and results.
