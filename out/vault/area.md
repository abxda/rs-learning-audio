# Area / Área

> **level 0** · *metric* · sources: th_validation

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The spatial extent of [[land|land]], [[crop|crop]] [[cover|cover]], or other geographic [[feature|feature]], often measured in [[hectares|hectares]] or square meters for [[statistical|statistical]] [[estimation|estimation]].
- **ES.** Extensión espacial de [[land|tierra]], [[cover|cobertura]] de [[crop|cultivo]] u otro [[feature|atributo]] geográfico, a menudo [[measure|medida]] en [[hectares|hectáreas]] o metros cuadrados para [[estimation|estimación]] estadística.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[land|Land]] — Area quantifies the spatial extent of land cover classes or agricultural fields.
- [[map|Map]] — Area can be estimated by counting pixels in a classified map or summing polygon extents.
- [[regions|Regions]] — Regions are geographic areas defined by boundaries, often used as study areas for mapping.
- [[study|Study]] — A study is a systematic investigation within a defined area to develop or contribute knowledge.
- [[agricultural-statistics|Agricultural Statistics]] — Agricultural statistics include area estimates of crop cultivation derived from satellite and ground data.
- [[area-statistics|Area statistics]] — Area statistics quantify the spatial extent of land cover classes from maps.
- [[research-areas|Research areas]] — Research areas define the spatial extent of interest for a remote sensing investigation.
- [[area-measurements|Area measurements]] — Area measurements quantify the spatial extent of land cover or crop types, often in hectares.
- [[planted-area|Planted area]] — Knowing the spatial extent, planted area measures the portion of land actually sown with a crop type.
- [[geographic-distribution|Geographic distribution]] — Geographic distribution describes how a variable's values are spread across spatial coordinates within a region.
- [[area-weighted-error-matrices|Area-Weighted Error Matrices]] — Area weighting adjusts confusion matrices by the spatial extent of each class to reduce bias in accuracy metrics.
- [[pixel-counting|Pixel counting]] — From a classified map, pixel counting yields the area for each land cover or crop type.
- [[crop-area|Crop area]] — Crop area is the spatial extent of land planted with a specific crop, often measured in hectares.
