# Vegetation / Vegetación

> **level 3** · *phenomenon* · sources: th_remote_sensing#introduction-to-remote-sensing-imagery

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Plant [[cover|cover]] on the [[earth|Earth]]'s surface, often monitored using [[indices|indices]] like [[ndvi|NDVI]] to assess health and [[growth|growth]].
- **ES.** [[cover|Cobertura]] vegetal en la superficie de la [[land|Tierra]], a menudo monitoreada mediante [[indices|índices]] como [[ndvi|NDVI]] para evaluar la salud y el [[growth|crecimiento]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[land|Land]] — *phenomenon*
    - **EN.** Vegetation is plant cover on Earth's surface, a key land cover type monitored via remote sensing.
    - **ES.** La vegetación es la cobertura vegetal en la superficie de la Tierra, un tipo clave de cobertura del suelo monitoreado mediante teledetección.
- [[cover|Cover]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** Vegetation is a type of cover on the Earth's surface, monitored using spectral indices.
    - **ES.** La vegetación es un tipo de cobertura en la superficie de la Tierra, monitoreada mediante índices espectrales.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[vegetation-indices|Vegetation indices]] — Vegetation indices use spectral combinations to quantify green biomass and plant condition.
- [[biophysical|Biophysical]] — Biophysical properties describe the physical and biological characteristics of vegetation, such as biomass and LAI.
- [[agronomic-conditions|Agronomic Conditions]] — Agronomic conditions encompass the physical environment and plant health factors influencing crop development.
- [[crop-conditions|Crop conditions]] — By analyzing spectral indices from satellite imagery, you assess crop conditions such as health and stress.
- [[vegetation-health|Vegetation health]] — Vegetation health is assessed through remote sensing spectral indices that measure plant vigor.
- [[biomass|Biomass]] — Biomass is the total mass of living vegetation in a given area, estimated from spectral indices.
- [[leaf-area-index|Leaf area index]] — Leaf area index measures the leaf area per ground unit, a key biophysical parameter of vegetation.
- [[ndvi|NDVI]] — NDVI is a spectral index designed to highlight vegetation, building on the concept of vegetation cover.
