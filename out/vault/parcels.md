# Parcels / Parcelas

> **level 3** · *data* · sources: ct_poland

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Individual [[agricultural|agricultural]] fields or plots, the fundamental spatial units for [[crop-mapping|crop mapping]] and [[area-estimation|area estimation]].
- **ES.** Campos o [[agricultural-plots|parcelas agrícolas]] individuales, las unidades espaciales fundamentales para el [[crop-mapping|mapeo de cultivos]] y la [[area-estimation|estimación de área]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[field|Field]] — *phenomenon*
    - **EN.** Parcels are individual agricultural fields or plots, the fundamental spatial units for crop mapping.
    - **ES.** Las parcelas son campos o lotes agrícolas individuales, las unidades espaciales fundamentales para el mapeo de cultivos.

## Lineage to fundamentals / Linaje hasta los fundamentos
[[area|Area]] → [[land|Land]] → [[field|Field]] → [[parcels|Parcels]]

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[polygons|Polygons]] — Parcels are often represented as polygons in geographic information systems for analysis.
- [[uhr-cfp-dataset|UHR CFP dataset]] — Each image in the UHR CFP dataset is paired with polygon labels of individual agricultural field parcels.
- [[agricultural-plots|Agricultural plots]] — Agricultural plots are the fundamental spatial units also known as parcels in crop mapping.
- [[agricultural-holding|Agricultural holding]] — An agricultural holding may consist of multiple parcels, all under unified economic management.
