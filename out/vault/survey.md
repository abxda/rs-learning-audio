# Survey / Encuesta

> **level 1** · *method* · sources: th_alignment#annual-agricultural-survey-2018

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A systematic [[collection|collection]] of [[ground|ground]] [[data|data]], often through [[field|field]] visits or questionnaires, used for [[validation|validation]] or [[statistical|statistical]] [[estimation|estimation]].
- **ES.** Una [[harvest|recolección]] sistemática de [[ground-truth-data|datos de campo]], a menudo mediante visitas de [[field|campo]] o cuestionarios, utilizada para [[validation|validación]] o [[estimation|estimación]] estadística.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[data|Data]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** A survey systematically collects ground data, such as field visits, for validation or analysis.
    - **ES.** Una encuesta recolecta sistemáticamente datos de campo, como visitas a campo, para validación o análisis.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[field-surveys|Field surveys]] — Field surveys are on-the-ground surveys that collect detailed crop information for training and validation.
- [[ground-survey-estimators|Ground survey estimators]] — Ground survey estimators use data from field surveys to produce unbiased population estimates.
- [[ground-surveys|Ground surveys]] — Ground surveys are field-based data collection campaigns that provide ground truth for remote sensing.
- [[agricultural-surveys|Agricultural surveys]] — Agricultural surveys are systematic survey programs that gather crop area and yield data, extending the general survey method to a specific agricultural context.
- [[survey-vector|Survey vector]] — A survey vector represents the crop type observed at a pixel, with element 1 for the observed crop and 0 for others.
- [[surveying|Surveying]] — Surveying is the systematic act of conducting a survey to gather data.
- [[statistical-survey|Statistical survey]] — A statistical survey is a systematic survey designed to produce quantitative estimates with known error.
- [[survey-records|Survey records]] — Survey records are the individual data entries collected during a systematic field survey.
