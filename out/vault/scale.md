# Scale / Escala

> **level 0** · *metric* · sources: th_remote_sensing · th_data_cubes

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The spatial or [[temporal-resolution|temporal resolution]] at which [[data|data]] are collected or analyzed, affecting the [[level|level]] of detail and the ability to detect [[patterns|patterns]], such as [[field|field]]-level versus regional scales.
- **ES.** La [[resolution|resolución]] espacial o temporal a la que se recopilan o analizan los [[data|datos]], que afecta el [[level|nivel]] de detalle y la capacidad de detectar [[patterns|patrones]], como escalas a nivel de [[field|campo]] versus regionales.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[national-scale-applications|National scale applications]] — National-scale applications apply remote sensing methods across an entire country for official statistics.
