# Earth / Tierra

> **level 2** · *phenomenon* · sources: th_remote_sensing · introduction

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** The [[planet|planet]] itself, often referred to in this [[handbook|handbook]] as the object of [[observation|observation]] via [[satellite-imagery|satellite imagery]], including its [[land|land]], water, and atmosphere.
- **ES.** El planeta mismo, a menudo referido en este [[handbook|manual]] como el objeto de [[observation|observación]] mediante [[satellite-imagery|imágenes satelitales]], incluyendo su [[land|tierra]], agua y atmósfera.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[land|Land]] — *phenomenon*
    - **EN.** Earth is the planet, including its land cover and surface, as observed by satellite imagery.
    - **ES.** La Tierra es el planeta, incluyendo su cobertura del suelo y superficie, observado mediante imágenes satelitales.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[soil|Soil]] — Soil forms from the weathering of Earth's crust and organic matter decomposition.
- [[remote-sensing-science|Remote Sensing Science]] — It studies Earth observation from a distance using sensors and energy interactions.
- [[earth-observation|Earth Observation]] — Earth Observation focuses on collecting data about Earth using satellite-mounted sensors.
