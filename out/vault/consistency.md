# Consistency / Consistencia

> **level 0** · *primitive*

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Property of being coherent or uniform across different observations or times.
- **ES.** Propiedad de ser coherente o uniforme a través de diferentes observaciones o momentos.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[standardization|Standardization]] — Standardization establishes uniform rules to ensure consistency across data and methods.
- [[reliability|Reliability]] — Reliability of a method is the consistency of its results across different datasets or conditions.
