# Observation / Observación

> **level 1** · *data* · sources: th_data_sources#accessing-ard-image-collections-in-cloud-providers

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A single [[data-point|data point]] acquired by a [[sensors|sensor]] at a specific [[time|time]] and location, e.g., a [[satellite-imagery|satellite image]] acquisition or [[ground|ground]] measurement.
- **ES.** Un único [[data-point|punto de datos]] adquirido por un sensor en un momento y lugar específicos, por [[example|ejemplo]], una adquisición de imagen satelital o una medición de [[field|campo]].

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[data|Data]] — *primitive*
    - **EN.** An observation is a single data point from a sensor, e.g., a satellite image acquisition.
    - **ES.** Una observación es un punto de datos individual de un sensor, por ejemplo, una adquisición de imagen satelital.

## Lineage to fundamentals / Linaje hasta los fundamentos
[[data|Data]] → [[observation|Observation]]

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[monitoring|Monitoring]] — Monitoring relies on a series of observations at regular intervals to track conditions.
- [[ground|Ground]] — Ground observations are measurements taken directly on the Earth's surface, such as crop type and yield data.
- [[instances|Instances]] — An instance can be an observation; a collection of instances forms a dataset for machine learning.
