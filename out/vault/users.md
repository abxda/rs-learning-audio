# Users / Usuarios

> **level 0** · *primitive* · sources: introduction · ad_learning

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Individuals or organizations that apply the [[methods|methods]] and [[tools|tools]] described in the [[handbook|handbook]], including [[national-statistical-offices|national statistical offices]], researchers, and [[policymakers|policymakers]] who need [[agricultural-statistics|agricultural statistics]].
- **ES.** Individuos u organizaciones que aplican los [[methods|métodos]] y [[tools|herramientas]] descritos en el [[handbook|manual]], incluyendo [[national-statistical-offices|oficinas nacionales de estadística]], investigadores y [[policymakers|formuladores de políticas]] que necesitan [[agricultural-statistics|estadísticas agrícolas]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[national-statistical-offices|National Statistical Offices]] — Users, especially national statistical offices, apply remote sensing methods to produce official agricultural statistics.
