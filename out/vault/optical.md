# Optical / Óptico

> **level 5** · *sensor* · sources: th_remote_sensing

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A [[type|type]] of [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]] [[sensors|sensor]] that measures reflected solar [[radiation|radiation]] in visible, [[near-infrared|near-infrared]], and shortwave infrared wavelengths. It is passive and requires sunlight.
- **ES.** [[type|Tipo]] de sensor de [[remote-sensing|teledetección]] que mide la [[radiation|radiación]] solar reflejada en longitudes de onda visibles, [[near-infrared|infrarrojo cercano]] e infrarrojo de onda corta. Es pasivo y requiere luz solar.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[satellite-sensors|Satellite sensors]] — *sensor*
    - **EN.** Optical sensors on satellites measure reflected solar radiation in these wavelength ranges.
    - **ES.** Los sensores ópticos en satélites miden la radiación solar reflejada en estos rangos de longitud de onda.
