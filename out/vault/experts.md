# Experts / Expertos

> **level 0** · *primitive* · sources: about · th_quality_control

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Individuals with specialized knowledge and experience in a domain, such as [[remote-sensing|remote sensing]], [[agriculture|agriculture]], or [[statistics|statistics]], who provide judgment, [[training-dataset|training data]] interpretation, or [[validation|validation]].
- **ES.** Individuos con conocimiento especializado y experiencia en un dominio, como [[remote-sensing|teledetección]], [[agriculture|agricultura]] o estadística, que brindan criterio, interpretación de [[training-dataset|datos de entrenamiento]] o [[validation|validación]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[regional-field-experts|Regional field experts]] — Experts are individuals like regional field experts with specialized knowledge in a domain.
- [[statisticians|Statisticians]] — They are experts who ensure that estimation methods are design-consistent and provide valid confidence intervals.
