# Robust sampling frame / Marco muestral robusto

> **level 0** · *method* · sources: th_design_frames

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A [[sampling-frame|sampling frame]] that is complete, accurate, and up-to-date, minimizing [[bias|bias]] and ensuring the [[samples|sample]] represents the target [[population|population]] well.
- **ES.** Un [[sampling-frame|marco de muestreo]] que es completo, preciso y actualizado, minimizando el [[bias|sesgo]] y asegurando que la muestra represente bien a la [[population|población]] objetivo.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[sampling-frame|Sampling frame]] — A robust sampling frame is a sampling frame that is complete, accurate, and up-to-date.
- [[representativeness|Representativeness]] — Robust sampling frames ensure representativeness of the target population for valid inference.
