# Sensors / Sensores

> **level 3** · *sensor* · sources: th_remote_sensing · th_data_sources

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** Instruments on aerial or space [[platforms|platforms]] that detect [[electromagnetic-radiation|electromagnetic radiation]] reflected or emitted from the [[earth|Earth]]'s surface, recording it as [[imagery|digital imagery]].
- **ES.** Instrumentos en [[platforms|plataformas]] aéreas o espaciales que detectan la [[electromagnetic-radiation|radiación electromagnética]] reflejada o emitida desde la superficie de la [[land|Tierra]], registrándola como [[images|imágenes]] digitales.

## Understand these first / Entiende esto primero
- [[satellite|Satellite]] — *platform*
    - **EN.** Sensors are the instruments mounted on satellites that collect imagery and other measurements.
    - **ES.** Los sensores son los instrumentos montados en satélites que recolectan imágenes y otras mediciones.
- [[remote-sensing|Remote sensing]] — *method*
    - **EN.** Remote sensing uses sensors on satellites or aircraft to detect electromagnetic radiation.
    - **ES.** La teledetección utiliza sensores en satélites o aeronaves para detectar radiación electromagnética.

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[radar|Radar]] — Radar sensors on satellites transmit pulses and record echoes to measure surface properties.
- [[satellite-sensors|Satellite sensors]] — Satellite sensors are specific sensors designed to operate in space and collect Earth observation data.
- [[spectral-channels|Spectral channels]] — Each spectral channel captures reflectance in a specific wavelength range determined by the sensor's filter.
- [[spectral-range|Spectral Range]] — Spectral range defines the wavelength intervals a sensor can detect, such as visible or near-infrared.
- [[satellite-imagery|Satellite imagery]] — Satellite imagery is produced by sensors aboard satellites that detect electromagnetic radiation.
