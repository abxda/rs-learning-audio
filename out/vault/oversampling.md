# Oversampling / Sobremuestreo

> **level 0** · *method*

**What it is / Qué es.**

- **EN.** A technique to increase the [[number|number]] of minority [[classes|class]] [[samples|samples]], often by generating synthetic [[data|data]], to correct class imbalance in [[training|training]] sets.
- **ES.** Técnica que incrementa el [[number|número]] de [[samples|muestras]] de [[classes|clases]] minoritarias, a menudo generando [[data|datos]] sintéticos, para corregir el desbalance de clases en los conjuntos de [[training|entrenamiento]].

## This unlocks / Esto habilita
- [[smote|SMOTE]] — SMOTE oversamples minority classes by generating synthetic examples, balancing imbalanced training datasets.
